Reporting Vulnerabilities

Please use our private security mailing list, security@trafficcontrol.apache.org, to disclose any new vulnerability. Disclosing vulnerabilities privately will allow our project team to analyze the report, identify a fix, and begin the full disclosure process. Please include all relevant information to reproduce the issue, and any known workaround or fix.
