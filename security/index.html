Reporting Vulnerabilities

Please use our private security mailing list, security@trafficcontrol.incubator.apache.org, to disclose any new vulnerability. Disclosing vulnerabilities privately will allow our project team to analyze the report, identify a fix, and begin the full disclosure process. Please include all relevant information to reproduce the issue, and any known workaround or fix.


Apache Traffic Control is an effort undergoing Incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
