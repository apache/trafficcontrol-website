Fall 2016 Meetup

On October 24-25, 2016, a Traffic Control Meetup was held in Sunnyvale, California. This meetup was free but we asked everyone to RSVP.

Agenda

  • Bug Scrub
  • Pull Request Reviews
  • The Apache Way
